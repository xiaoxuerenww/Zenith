## 📦 Package Contents

### Core Skills

#### 1. Daily Briefing Skill
- **Location:** `skills/daily-briefing/`
- **Function:** 20 curated items (5 per category) from Politics, Tech, Economics, LLM Research
- **Features:**
  - Importance Level (CRITICAL/HIGH/MEDIUM/EMERGING)
  - Sentiment Analysis (Positive/Negative/Neutral)
  - Impact Score (1-10)
  - Real working links
  - Professional formatting
  - Daily at 8 AM or on-demand
  - Email subscription ready

#### 2. Paper Crawler Skill (Research Frontier)
- **Location:** `skills/paper-crawler/`
- **Function:** Top 5 papers in LLM × Recommendation Systems
- **Features:**
  - Multi-source discovery (ArXiv, Papers with Code, Scholar)
  - Relevance scoring (0-100)
  - Complete paper metadata
  - Daily at 8 AM or on-demand
  - Markdown/HTML/JSON output formats

## ✨ Unique Features
