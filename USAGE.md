# Usage Guide — Zenith Skills

## Daily Briefing

### Basic Usage

**Trigger phrases (any of these work):**
```
"Set up my daily morning briefing"
"Create a daily digest of top stories"
"I want focused intelligence across multiple domains"
"Automate my news research"
"Start my daily briefing"
```

### What You Get

**Default Delivery:**
- 20 curated items (5 per category)
- Professional HTML or Word format
- Delivered daily at 8 AM
- Each item includes:
  - Title
  - Source attribution
  - Date (Month DD, YYYY)
  - 2-3 sentence summary
  - Direct link to full article
  - Importance level (CRITICAL/HIGH/MEDIUM/EMERGING)
  - Sentiment (Positive/Negative/Neutral)
  - Impact score (1-10)

**Time to Read:** ~5-10 minutes for full brief

### Customization Examples

**Change the domains:**
```
"Set up briefing with only tech and politics"
"Give me AI, startups, and cryptocurrency news"
"I want health, science, and environmental coverage"
```

**Change the amount:**
```
"Give me 3 items per category instead of 5"
"Show me top 10 items total"
"I want just the single most important story per domain"
```

**Change the schedule:**
```
"Run my briefing at 6 AM instead of 8 AM"
"Give me briefings twice daily: 8 AM and 6 PM"
"Just run it on-demand when I ask"
```

**Change the format:**
```
"Send briefing as a Word document"
"Generate HTML format"
"Format as JSON for my dashboard"
"Just plain markdown text"
```

**Filter by importance:**
```
"Only show CRITICAL items"
"Skip neutral items, just positive and negative"
"Focus on high-impact developments (8+ impact score)"
```

## Research Frontier (Paper Crawler)

### Basic Usage

**Trigger phrases (any of these work):**
```
"Set up a daily paper digest on LLM and recommendation systems"
"Find the latest academic papers on LLM-based recommendations"
"Create my research brief automation"
"Start crawling latest papers in my field"
"Set up daily research updates"
```

### What You Get

**Default Delivery:**
- Top 5 papers per day
- From: ArXiv, Papers with Code, Google Scholar
- Scheduled daily at 8 AM
- Each paper includes:
  - Title (exact from source)
  - Authors (first 3-4, then et al.)
  - Publication date
  - 1-2 sentence summary

**Time to Read:** ~5-10 minutes

**Change the schedule:**

## Advanced Features

### Analytics Deep Dive

Each briefing item includes 3 analytics:

**1. Importance Level**
- Use this to filter by urgency
- CRITICAL items need attention today
- MEDIUM items are informational

**2. Sentiment**
- Positive: Good news, growth, optimism
- Negative: Risks, downturns, concerns
- Neutral: Factual developments

**3. Impact Score (1-10)**
- 9-10: Market-moving, field-defining
- 7-8: Significant ripple effects
- 5-6: Important but localized
- 3-4: Noteworthy developments
- 1-2: Emerging or minor stories

### Using the Analytics

**For decision making:**
```
"Show me only CRITICAL items with 8+ impact score"
"What are the negative developments I should know about?"
"Which positive stories are driving markets?"
```

**For pattern recognition:**
```
"Are most tech stories positive or negative this week?"
"What's the median impact score across domains?"
"Is sentiment shifting in any category?"
```

## Tips & Best Practices

### 1. Set a Consistent Time
Pick one time each day and stick to it. Your brain will anticipate the briefing.

### 2. Review Analytics
Don't just skim titles. The Impact and Sentiment scores add valuable context.

### 3. Follow the Links
The summaries are 2-3 sentences. Full articles have the complete story.

### 4. Use On-Demand for Breaking News
Don't wait for the scheduled briefing if something major happens.

### 5. Customize Your Domains
The default 4 domains work for most, but personalize to what matters to you.

### 6. Build a Routine
Pair your briefing with morning coffee or end-of-day review.

### 7. Share Interesting Items
The links are clickable—forward important stories to colleagues.

## Troubleshooting Usage

### "Briefing took too long"
- Try with fewer categories (3 instead of 4)
- Request on-demand instead of scheduling
- Check your internet connection

### "Links don't work in examples"
- Examples use sample links
- Live briefings have real, working links
- Only occurs in example/demo outputs

### "I want different information"
- You can customize everything
- See "Customization Examples" above
- Ask the skill to adjust anytime

### "Can I get more/less detail?"
```
"Give me longer summaries"
"Make the summaries shorter"
"Include more context"
"Just the headlines"
```
